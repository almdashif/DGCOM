Product Range

Ecological Asset Mapping & Analytics

We offer a robust framework for assessing the spatial extent and health status of key ecological components such as woodlands, wetlands, and agricultural lands. Our solutions enable early detection of stress in trees due to climatic factors, early detection of infestations, assessing dryness stress in wetlands, and mapping the spatial spread of invasive species. This helps firms efficiently manage natural habitats. We provide timely intelligence, allowing firms to plan at the micro level and manage their land, agricultural, and wetland features better.

Tree species detection

Our Tree Species Detection feature combines sophisticated machine learning algorithms with robust data visualization tools to deliver precise and actionable insights into tree biodiversity. The system identifies and classifies tree species from images, presenting the results through interactive data visualizations and detailed tables.

Tree Height Estimation

The Tree Height Generation feature provides accurate and reliable measurements of tree heights using advanced imaging technology and algorithms. By analyzing high-resolution images or lidar data, our system estimates the height of trees with precision, offering valuable insights for plantation and forestry management, carbon sequestration, commercial log planning, environmental studies, and land use planning.

Carbon Mapping and Natural Resources Tracking

Our Carbon Mapping and Natural Resources Tracking service provides precise and comprehensive monitoring of natural and environmental resources. Leveraging cutting-edge technology, we deliver critical insights to support sustainable management and strategic decision-making.

Key Features:


⦁ Natural Resources Tracking: Monitor changes in forests, vegetation cover, agricultural fields, and water bodies with exceptional precision.
⦁ Environmental Features Monitoring: Track changes in land use, land cover, and urban sprawl to gain a deeper understanding of environmental dynamics.
⦁ Carbon Credit Opportunities: Identify and capitalize on carbon credit opportunities through accurate carbon mapping. Our service helps you measure carbon sequestration and emissions, facilitating participation in carbon markets and supporting sustainability goals.
⦁ Organization and User Management: Efficiently manage organizational data and user access to streamline operations.

Tree Age Estimation

The attribution of plantation/tree age assessment data helps in the decision of ecological assets for commercial and conservation purposes. The age feature aids us in strategizing the provision of natural resources for sustainable industrial development.

Water Stress

The Water Stress Monitoring feature analyzes plant and tree health to assess water stress levels using image analysis and environmental data. By evaluating physiological secondary indicators and leveraging historical climate variables, this feature helps in detection of water stress in vegetation. This stress alarm initiates timely response for improving the tree survival rate.

Crop Growth

The Crop Growth Monitoring feature provides comprehensive insights into the development and health of crops through advanced imaging and data analytics. By tracking various growth parameters and analyzing visual and environmental data, this feature helps optimize crop management, strategies, and plans to increase yields, and ensure healthy plant development.

Disaster Management​

Our Disaster Management solutions provide comprehensive monitoring and analysis to protect your assets from natural disasters and other threats. With advanced technologies, we deliver critical insights and timely responses to ensure the safety and integrity of your operations.

Key Features:


⦁ Disaster Monitoring: Access damage and changes caused by natural disasters such as floods, earthquakes, and wildfires
⦁ Asset Timely Analysis: Provide timely analysis of changes occurring around assets of interest to enable rapid response.
⦁ Asset Intrusion Monitoring: Detect and monitor intrusions around critical assets to prompt pre-emptive actions and enhance security.
